Honey-Glazed Salmon

Description

This is a quick, easy recipe with delicious results. Honey-glazed salmon with a tasty lemon-butter sauce and a garlic kick. It pairs great with some asparagus and rice, or even better, a white wine.

Ingredients

  • 2 (6 oz) salmon filets
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp parsley

Steps

  1. Thaw salmon for 30-60 minutes in cold water.
  2. Marinate salmon in lemon juice for 10+ minutes in refrigerator.
  3. Remove salmon and pat dry with paper towels. Evenly coat both sides with flour, then drizzle approximately 1 tablespoon of honey on each side.
  4. Coat a large nonstick pan with cooking spray or olive oil. Place both pieces of salmon in pan and cook over medium heat, 3-5 minutes per side.
  5. While salmon is cooking, prepare sauce. Place butter in a small saucepan over low-medium heat, swirling occasionally, until melted. Stir in lemon juice, garlic salt, and pepper. Stir often and continue to saute for 1 minute. Rewarm if needed.
  6. When salmon is fully cooked, plate and cover each fillet with sauce. Serve immediately.
